What This Ebook Is About

Home Assistant is a hands-on, practical guide to mastering open-source home automation. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced maker, this book introduces you to the powerful, free, and open-source Home Assistant platform, empowering you to take full control of your smart home.

Learn how to configure sensors, automate your home, and create custom solutions using affordable hardware like the ESP8266. Save energy, reduce costs, and break free from vendor lock-ins with this comprehensive guide.

  • Configuring sensors, actuators, and automation scripts
  • Building custom sensors with affordable hardware like the ESP8266
  • Saving energy and money with smart energy management

Home automation is transforming the way we live. With open-source platforms like Home Assistant, you can:
